3. Each team will be given “Clues” at the beginning of the treasure hunt. All of the treasure hunt clues will be in a paper. 4. Teams will be scored on the total value of the clues collected and the amount of time taken. Once all coins are collected teams must return to the finish in order to be counted as finished. Clue values will be determined based on the difficulty of the clue.
5. Each team in the hunt is assigned a number. A paper with this number on it is provided to you at sign in on the day of the hunt. It will be used by Hunt coordinators to spot check compliance with the safety rules.
6. “Clues” will be distributed to team members at that time and may be opened and the teams may start solving the clues and retrieving “Treasure Hunt Clues”.
7. Disqualification will result from any of the following
a. Damaging any property
b. Interfering with other teams or their members in any way
c. Copying from other teams.

Slow Drag
1. Riders must wear proper ISO approved helmets.
2. Riders must stay in their lane and cannot change the lane.
3. Riders must not touch the ground with their feet after the start of the race.
4. Any participant who rides out of the lane or touches the ground is disqualified.
5. Last one to cross the finish line wins.
6. In case no rider crosses the finish line the last rider standing before being disqualified wins.
7. First prize will be awarded to the last one standing.
